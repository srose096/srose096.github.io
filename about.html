About Us

Welcome to GeeGees Golf & Country, a premier golf course that offers a unique and enjoyable experience for golfers of all levels. Nestled in a picturesque location, our course provides stunning views and a challenging yet rewarding playing experience. Our dedicated team is committed to providing exceptional service and maintaining the course to the highest standards. We offer a range of services, including golf lessons, driving range facilities, and a fully stocked pro shop with the latest golf equipment and apparel. Whether you're a seasoned golfer or just starting out, our course offers something for everyone. Join us for a round of golf, improve your skills with our professional instructors, or simply relax and enjoy the beautiful surroundings. We look forward to welcoming you to GeeGees Golf & Country and providing you with an unforgettable golfing experience.
